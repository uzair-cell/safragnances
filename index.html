Our Story

Founded by Saad Abbasi, SA Fragrances brings you the finest Islamic perfumes crafted with traditional methods and premium ingredients. Our scents are alcohol-free, long-lasting, and inspired by the rich heritage of Arabian perfumery.

Each fragrance is carefully blended to evoke memories of sacred places and timeless traditions, while meeting the highest standards of modern perfumery.
